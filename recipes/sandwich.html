Reuben Sandwich

Description

Simple, delicious sandwich for a tasty lunch. Makes for a quick snack or can be paired with your choice of soup. Cook time is 10 minutes. Preparation time is 15 minutes. This recipe makes us a total of 4 servings.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablspoons butter
  • 8 slices rye bread
  • 8 slices deli sliced corned beef
  • 8 slices Swiss cheese
  • 1 cup sauerkraut, drained
  • 1/2 cup Thousand Islands dressing
