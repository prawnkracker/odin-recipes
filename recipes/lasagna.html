Hearty Vegetable Lasagna

Description

A quick and easy recipe for a Hearty Vegetable Lasagna. The cook time for this recipe is a total of 1 hour. The preparation time is 40 minutes. This delicious meal serves a total of 12 servings. Perfect for having guests over for a nice relaxing night with a healthy meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 Oz.) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 lbs. fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 3/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 3/4 cup chopped onion
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 (26 Oz.) jars pasta sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 (15 Oz.) container part-skim ricotta cheese
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
